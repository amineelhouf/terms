Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the “Amine TikTok Automation” app (“the App”) handles information when connecting to TikTok using OAuth. The App is operated by Amine Elhouf (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

1. Information we collect

When you authorize the App with TikTok, we may receive the following information from TikTok, depending on the scopes you approve:

  • Basic profile information (such as your TikTok user ID).
  • Permissions needed to upload videos to your TikTok account.
  • Access tokens required to perform authorized actions.

2. How we use this information

We use the information only to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth.
  • Upload videos that you have prepared and approved.
  • Maintain the technical operation and security of the automation workflow.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information with any third parties.

3. Data storage

Access tokens may be stored securely in our automation tool (n8n) only for as long as needed to perform the publishing workflow. Tokens are not used for any purpose other than the automation described in this Policy.

4. Third-party services

The App uses TikTok’s official APIs and may rely on n8n.cloud or a self-hosted n8n instance to run automations. These services have their own privacy policies and security practices, which we do not control.

5. Your choices

  • You can revoke the App’s access to your TikTok account at any time from your TikTok settings.
  • You may contact us to request deletion of stored tokens associated with your account.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for use by adults and is not directed to children under the age allowed by TikTok’s Terms of Service.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

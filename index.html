TikTok OAuth App

Amine TikTok Automation

This website is used to host the legal pages and OAuth verification for Amine's TikTok automation app.

What this app does

The app helps post short Moroccan comedy videos to TikTok using a secure OAuth connection. Videos are prepared by the creator and published through an automation workflow (n8n) only after explicit user authorization.

Data and permissions

The app only requests the minimum TikTok scopes required to upload videos to the creator's own TikTok account. It does not sell or share data with third parties.
